Service Delivery Modes

We offer three flexible models. Our most popular is our Cloud-Based (Online) Mode.

1. Online / Cloud-Based Accounting

Designed to save resources, office space, and money while maintaining real-time accuracy.

Cloud Integration

We host your software (Tally) on a secure cloud. You provide a User ID and Password.

Document Sharing

Share purchase bills and statements via Google Drive or Email daily.

Real-Time Entry

Transactions are recorded daily, ensuring books are always up to date.

Sales & Logistics

Inform us of a sale; we generate Sales Invoices and E-Way Bills immediately.

Why choose Online Mode?

It eliminates the need for in-house accounting staff and physical storage, providing a highly efficient, "paperless" financial ecosystem.

2. Offline / On-Site Accounting Mode

For businesses that prefer physical presence and in-office management, we offer flexible on-site support by our accounting professionals.

Flexible Visit Options

  • Part-Time Basis: Fixed daily hours as per prior agreement.
  • Full-Day Basis: Entire day support charged based on requirements.

Scope & Filings

  • On-Site Data Entry: Dedicated entry and ledger management at your location.
  • Statutory Filings: GST, TDS, and ITR filings are available as an add-on service.
  • Variable Pricing: Filing charges are calculated based on data volume and return frequency.
